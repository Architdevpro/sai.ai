SAI Features

SAI offers a wide range of features designed to make learning interactive, personalized, and fun for students, teachers, and parents.

AI-Generated Sample Papers:

Practice with customized sample papers for all main subjects, tailored for your grade.

Interactive Quizzes:

Test your knowledge with instant feedback and progress tracking.

Full-Length Tests:

Simulate real exam conditions with leaderboards and analytics.

Hyper-Adaptive Learning Paths:

AI adapts the content based on your performance for focused improvement.

Real-Time Doubt Resolver:

Instant help for any academic question, anytime!

Smart Predictive Analytics:

Advanced dashboards predict performance and highlight focus areas.

Gamified Learning System:

Earn points, badges, and levels to stay motivated.

Admin Panel:

Educators and administrators can manage and monitor student progress efficiently.

🚀 Revolutionary New Feature: Context-Aware AI Assistance

  • In-Quiz AI Help: Get hints or explanations during quizzes without leaving your session.
  • In-Test AI Support: Real-time tutoring available during tests (if enabled by your admin).
  • Sample Paper AI Tutor: Receive instant help on any question in sample papers.
  • Admin Control: Teachers can enable/disable AI assistance for each quiz or test as needed.
  • Context-Aware Learning: AI understands the question you’re working on and provides tailored support.
